How to Delete Your Account

Steps to Delete Your Account:

  1. Log into your account on the app.
  2. Go to the **Settings** section by clicking the gear icon in the top-right corner of the app.
  3. Scroll down to the **Account Management** section.
  4. Click on the **Delete Account** button.
  5. A confirmation prompt will appear. Review the information and click **Confirm** to delete your account.

Important Note: This action is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be permanently deleted from our system.
